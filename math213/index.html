  • The prerequisite topics for Math 213 are those in Math 212 as taught at CCNY since Fall 2019- see this description of 212 topics. If you are missing some of those topics from taking a course which is judged equivalent (generally having about 80% of the topics) it is a good idea to fill those gaps before the term starts as it will be difficult to pass the class without mastery of topics from 212 including
    • Vector operations including dot and cross product, with both algebraic and geometric understanding
    • Lines, planes, and parameterized curves, in the plane and in space.
    • Functions of several variables: limits, properties, and partial derivatives.
    • Classifying and sketching of surfaces in space, including cylinders and quadric surfaces
