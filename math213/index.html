Announcements:

  • As mentioned in class, for most students, the Pearson homework system is not the best way to learn the material and prepare for the exams. For most students, reading the text before the lecture, reading the text after the lecture, attempting old departmental final exams, and reviewing notes is more effective. The course has both conceptual and computational aspects and the HW emphasizes the computational aspects (grouped neatly into sections) at the expense of the concepts. Reviewing old final exams from 203 is generally good preparation for exams as an important skill is classifying which approach to take for problems.
