What Do I Need On The Final...

Enter your grades as numbers between 0 and 100. This program estimates the grade you need on the final to get an 'A', a 'B', or a 'C' in the course, based upon the criteria laid out in the syllabus from the first day of class. Among the first 3 exams, a later exam score can replace an earlier exam score, and this can happen at most once, in the way which beneifts you most.

Scores will all be out of 100.
